Frequently asked

Common questions

Anything else? Just send me a message.

Do I own the site after it's built?

Yes! 100%. Once the final payment is made, all source code, files, and credentials are yours. There are no recurring licensing fees from me.

What do I need to provide to get started?

Your brand assets (logo, colors, fonts if you have them), product details, and a general idea of what you want. I'll guide you through everything else during the consultation.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely. You can add features or upgrade your plan at any point. I'll quote the difference based on what needs to be added.

Which payment methods do you accept?

Bank transfer (Nigerian banks) and mobile money. Details are shared after we agree on the scope and quote.

What happens if the project takes longer than estimated?

If delays are on my end, there's no extra charge. If scope changes are requested mid-project that extend the timeline, we'll discuss and agree on any adjustments together.
